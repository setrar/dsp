
### Rules of derivatives

- [ ] Constant Rule

```math
\begin{gather}
\\
y = k       & \quad \to & \quad y^{'} = 0 \\
y = x^{n}  & \quad \to & \quad y^{'} = nx^{n - 1} \\
y = kx^{n} & \quad  \to & \quad y^{'} = knx^{n - 1} \\

\end{gather}
```  

- [ ] Power Rule

```math
y = uv & \quad \to  \quad y^{'} = u^{'}v + uv^{'}
```

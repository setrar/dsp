A_{m,n} = 
 \begin{pmatrix}

  a_{2,1} & a_{2,2} & \cdots & a_{2,n} \\
  \vdots  & \vdots  & \ddots & \vdots  \\
  a_{m,1} & a_{m,2} & \cdots & a_{m,n} 
 \end{pmatrix}

Recursive function

```math
f(n) =
  \begin{cases}
    1       & \quad \text{if } n = 0 \text{ or } n = 1 \\
    f(n - 2) + f(n -1)  & \quad \text{if } n > 1
  \end{cases}
```
